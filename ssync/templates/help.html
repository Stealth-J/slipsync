About SlipSync

SlipSync allows you to convert, preview, and share bet slips across supported platforms. It simplifies the process of moving your selections between bookmakers by ensuring compatibility where possible.

  • Supported Platforms: Bet9ja and SportyBet.
  • Supported Sport: Football only (no other sports at the moment).
  • Unsupported Games: Live bets, casino games, outrights, and player-based selections.
  • Limitations: Certain markets and leagues may not be convertible across platforms.
How to Use
  • Enter your slip code into the input box.
  • Select the platform the slip is from (e.g., Bet9ja or SportyBet).
  • Choose the platform you want to convert the slip to.
  • Preview the converted slip and review selections, odds, and availability.
  • Make adjustments if needed to ensure all selections are valid.
  • Book the bet once you have at least one valid selection.
