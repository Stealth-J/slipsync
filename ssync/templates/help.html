About SlipSync

SlipSync helps you convert, preview, and share bet slips across supported platforms. Enter your slip link, switch platforms using the arrows, and click convert to get started.

How to Use
  • Paste your slip link in the input box.
  • Use the left/right arrows to switch platforms.
  • Click "Convert Slip" to see results.
  • Review selections, total odds, and copy the new link if needed.
